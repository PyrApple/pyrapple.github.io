Anaglyph (WIP)

Anaglyph is a binaural spatialization plugin based on research conducted at the LAM-CNRS (IJLRA, Sorbonne Universit), with participation by the Dyson School of Design Eng. (Imperial College of London). I had the privilege to wrap all that good work into a neat little freeware VST using the JUCE framework.
