Square (2017 - 2023)

Square is a series of installations leading participants to explore a location through the ears (binaural composition) of someone else. The concept sprang from our work on interactive audio web interfaces during the CoSiMa project. Each iteration takes you to a different person, time, or place. Each has been a different adventure to which I've had the pleasure to partake under the direction of composer Lorenzo Bianchi Hoesch.
